Oh Christmas Tree

Posted on December 18th, 2016

Christmas would not be the same without a christmas tree. After having decorated my tree I have come up with steps so that you get your tree looking as amazing as possible.

  1. Set up your tree on the stand and add the skirt. Then separate the branches as best as possible
  2. Add on the lights starting from the top.
  3. Put your ornaments on branches, trying to spread them equally around the tree
  4. Last but not least, add the tinsel
